currently running

Math Contest Prep

A structured competition math program for students in elementary and middle school, built around the kinds of problems seen in competitions like MathCounts and AMC 8 — where creative thinking matters more than speed or memorization. The program is organized into 3 groups, with a full curriculum, online video lessons, and guided homework designed to build skills step by step. Students can also access extra help sessions for support when they get stuck.

Weekly · 3 groups · help sessions · always free
